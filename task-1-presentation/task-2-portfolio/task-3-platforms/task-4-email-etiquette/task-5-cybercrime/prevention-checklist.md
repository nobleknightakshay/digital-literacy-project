Stay Safe Online — Prevention Checklist:

(1) ✅ Never share your UPI PIN, OTP, or password with anyone — not even bank employees
(2) ✅ Remember: you do NOT need to scan a QR code or enter a PIN to receive money
(3) ✅ Always verify the identity of anyone contacting you claiming to be from a bank or government agency
(4) ✅ Use two-factor authentication (2FA) on all your accounts
(5) ✅ Do not click on unknown links received via SMS, WhatsApp, or email
(6) ✅ Regularly update your passwords and never reuse the same password across platforms
(7) ✅ Only download apps from official sources — Google Play Store or Apple App Store
(8) ✅ Lock your phone with a strong PIN or biometric authentication
(9) ✅ If you suspect fraud, immediately call the Cyber Crime Helpline: 1930 (available 24x7)
(10) ✅ Report any cybercrime at the National Cyber Crime Portal: cybercrime.gov.in
