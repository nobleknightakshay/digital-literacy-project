Social Media Do's and Don'ts checklist:

Social Media Do's ✅

(1) Keep your profiles professional and up to date
(2) Think before you post — once online, always online
(3) Use strong, unique passwords for each social media account
(4) Report any cyberbullying or suspicious activity immediately
(5) Give credit when sharing someone else's content or work

Social Media Don'ts ❌

(1) Never share personal information like your address, phone number, or Aadhaar details publicly
(2) Don't accept friend/follow requests from strangers
(3) Never post offensive, hateful, or misleading content
(4) Don't use the same password across multiple platforms
(5) Never click on suspicious links or pop-ups shared via social media
